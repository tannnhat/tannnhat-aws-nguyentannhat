## Overview

This section summarizes my learning journey throughout the **12-week First Cloud Journey (FCJ)** internship program. During the internship, I studied the fundamentals of Cloud Computing, explored Amazon Web Services (AWS), and completed hands-on laboratory exercises provided by the AWS Study Group.

The purpose of this worklog is to record my weekly learning progress, practical activities, knowledge gained, and the skills developed throughout the internship.

During the 12-week program, I:

- Learned the fundamental concepts of Cloud Computing and AWS.
- Became familiar with the AWS Management Console and AWS CLI.
- Practiced deploying and managing AWS resources.
- Explored core AWS services, including Compute, Storage, Networking, Database, and Security.
- Completed hands-on labs and practical exercises based on the AWS Study Group learning path.
- Improved my problem-solving abilities and practical cloud computing skills.

The internship lasted **12 weeks**, with each week focusing on different AWS topics and practical activities.
