> After participating in the **First Cloud AI Journey** program, I had many opportunities to learn, practice, and take part in community activities. Below are my personal evaluations and suggestions, which I hope will help improve the program and provide even greater value to future participants.

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Learning Environment**  
The learning environment of the First Cloud AI Journey program was friendly, professional, and motivating. The mentors and community members were always willing to help whenever I encountered difficulties while studying or completing the hands-on labs. The learning roadmap was well organized, making it easier to understand AWS Cloud concepts from basic to advanced levels.

**2. Support from Mentors and the FCJ Team**  
The mentors were enthusiastic and always willing to answer questions and share practical experience throughout the program. The FCJ team regularly updated learning materials, announcements, and schedules while providing timely support whenever participants needed assistance. I especially appreciated that the mentors encouraged us to think independently and solve problems before providing the final solution.

**3. Relevance to My Major**  
The program was highly relevant to my Information Technology major. In addition to strengthening my knowledge of Cloud Computing, it introduced me to modern technologies such as AWS, DevOps, Serverless Computing, Cloud Security, and Generative AI, giving me valuable practical experience beyond what I had learned at university.

**4. Learning and Skill Development Opportunities**  
Through hands-on labs, workshops, and Community Sharing Day, I gained valuable practical experience in deploying cloud infrastructure on AWS, using various AWS services, working with Docker, and understanding Serverless Architecture. I also improved my self-learning ability, problem-solving skills, teamwork, and professional communication.

**5. Community Culture and Team Spirit**  
One of the most impressive aspects of the program was the collaborative and supportive community. Mentors, speakers, and participants were always willing to share their knowledge and experiences, creating a positive learning environment where everyone could learn from each other. This motivated me to become more confident and proactive throughout the program.

**6. Program Activities and Benefits**  
The program offered not only hands-on labs but also technical workshops and Community Sharing Day sessions covering practical cloud technologies. These activities helped me stay up to date with the latest technology trends, learn from industry experts, and expand my professional network within the AWS community.

### Additional Questions

- **What was the most satisfying part of the program?**  
  The most rewarding experience was completing the hands-on AWS labs and attending technical workshops presented by experienced mentors and AWS professionals. These activities helped me understand how cloud technologies are applied in real-world projects.

- **What do you think could be improved for future participants?**  
  I would like to see more advanced hands-on labs covering topics such as Kubernetes, Amazon EKS, Amazon ECS, Terraform, and CI/CD. In addition, introducing larger team projects would allow participants to apply everything they have learned to a complete cloud solution.

- **Would you recommend this program to your friends? Why?**  
  Yes. I would definitely recommend the First Cloud AI Journey program because it provides high-quality learning materials, practical AWS experience, dedicated mentors, and a supportive learning community. It is an excellent starting point for students who want to pursue a career in Cloud Computing or become a Cloud Engineer.

### Suggestions and Expectations

- Organize more advanced workshops on AWS services and Cloud Native technologies.
- Invite more Cloud Engineers from industry to share their real-world experiences.
- Provide additional team-based projects to improve collaboration and project development skills.
- Continue updating the curriculum with the latest AWS services, AI technologies, and DevOps practices.
- I hope the First Cloud AI Journey program will continue to grow and provide more learning opportunities for students who are passionate about Cloud Computing.

> Overall, the **First Cloud AI Journey** program was an excellent learning experience with significant practical value. It not only helped me build a solid foundation in AWS Cloud Computing but also provided opportunities to learn from mentors, industry experts, and the AWS community. The knowledge and experience I gained throughout the program will be valuable assets as I continue my journey toward becoming a Cloud Engineer.
